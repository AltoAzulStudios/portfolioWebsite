Welcome!

Greetings, my name is C. Elliott Lawson, a game designer and developer based in the Netherlands.
It is my mission is to craft games that inspire, challenge, and resonate with players on a deeper level. Drawing from my diverse experiences, I believe in the power of games to tell meaningful stories, create unforgettable experiences, and connect people across the globe. As I continue to evolve as a developer, I am committed to delivering games that not only entertain but also leave a lasting impact on the gaming community and beyond.
On this website you will find a detailed layout of my work to showcase my abilities, along with, a list of my skills and professional references I've picked up over the years. If you are interested in a collaboration or want to know where to find my work please visit the Contact & Shop page as listed above!

About Me

My name is C. Elliott Lawson, a game developer and designer with a unique blend of experiences that have shaped my creative approach over the past decade. Before stepping into the world of game development, I spent 10 years as a mixologist and bar manager, where I mastered the art of crafting memorable experiences. This background not only honed my attention to detail and passion for storytelling but also pushed the boundaries of my creativity, enabling me to think outside the box and approach game design with a fresh perspective.
